videojuego top 5

  1. Mother 3
    • Great writing, with an interesting setting and slightly wacky yet relatable characters.
      Some of the best turn-based rpg combat, with dynamic roles for each party member.
      Really just a masterpiece through-and-through.

  2. Celeste
    • the only game i've ever seriously speedran. perfect platformer movement, great and
      challenging levels, and a cute story to boot.

  3. Rain World
    • Probably the most beautiful pixel art I've seen. Super interesting and unique survival gameplay,
      Expecting creativiy and mastery from the player and brutally punishing mistakes.
      Interesting lore and world, mixing post-apocolyptic, lovecraftian, and buddist-style imagery and themes.

  4. Nuclear Throne
    • Frantic, adrenaline-pumping, rewarding. Nuclear Throne nails that classic roguelike progression
      of sucking, then learing more and getting better till your finally overcome. Much has been said on the
      gamefeel, and that results in a game that just feels good in the hands. I just love how in ~15 minutes
      i can consistantly have such a fun and blood-pumping time.

  5. Super Smash Bros
